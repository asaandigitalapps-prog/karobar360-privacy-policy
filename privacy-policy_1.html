Introduction

Welcome to Karobar360, an AI-powered business and personal finance management application developed by Asaan Digital Applications, based in Pakistan.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Karobar360 mobile application. By using our app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy. Your financial data stays on your device and is never sold to third parties.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Name, mobile number, and password (encrypted) when you register
  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, invoices, and business transactions you manually enter
  • SMS Data (Optional): With your explicit consent, we read SMS messages to automatically detect bank transactions. This data is processed locally on your device.
  • Business Data: Business names, branch details, transaction types you create
  • Location Data (Optional): Only when you grant permission, used for location-based features
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Camera/Photos (Optional): Only when you choose to attach photos to invoices or transactions

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Karobar360 app features
  • Generate financial reports, income statements, and insights
  • Enable SMS-based automatic transaction detection (with your consent)
  • Send invoices and financial summaries via WhatsApp or SMS
  • Provide biometric authentication for app security
  • Backup and restore your data between devices
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
We never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data security is our top priority:

  • Local Storage: Most of your financial data is stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Backup: If you use backup features, data is securely stored using Firebase (Google) with encryption
  • Passwords: All passwords are encrypted and never stored in plain text
  • 4-Digit PIN: Your recovery PIN is encrypted for account recovery purposes only
  • Biometrics: Fingerprint/face data never leaves your device — processed by Android OS only
Pakistan Data: Your data is processed and stored in compliance with Pakistan's data protection guidelines.

SMS Permission

Karobar360 may request permission to read SMS messages. This permission is:

  • Optional — the app works fully without it
  • Used only to detect bank transaction SMS (Jazz Cash, EasyPaisa, bank alerts)
  • Processed locally on your device — SMS content is never sent to our servers
  • Revokable at any time from your device Settings
SMS data is only read when you actively use the SMS import feature and is never stored permanently.

Third-Party Services

Karobar360 uses these trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, cloud backup, and crash reporting
  • Google Play Services: App distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them at policies.google.com/privacy

Your Rights

As a Karobar360 user, you have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored in the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export your financial data as PDF or Excel
  • Revoke any permission (SMS, Camera, Location) from device settings
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your profile
To delete your account and all data, go to Profile → Delete Account inside the app.

Children's Privacy

Karobar360 is designed for business owners and adults aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify you through the app
  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will be changed
  • Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the new policy
